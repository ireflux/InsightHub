### Agent Safehouse – macOS-native sandboxing for local agents
A new tool named Agent Safehouse provides macOS-native sandboxing for local AI agents. It utilizes a deny-first access model enforced by the kernel to block unauthorized file access outside the designated project directory. Users can execute agents with restricted permissions via a single shell script without complex dependencies.
Source: [agent-safehouse.dev](https://agent-safehouse.dev/)

### Judges Find AI Doesn't Have Human Intelligence in Two New Court Cases
Two U.S. courts have ruled that AI systems do not possess human intelligence in separate copyright and legal privilege contexts. A judge rejected an attempt to copyright artwork created by an AI bot, citing regulations requiring works to owe their origin to a human being. Additionally, a judge ruled that a defendant could not claim attorney-client privilege for exchanges with an AI assistant during a criminal investigation.
Source: [yahoo.com](https://yahoo.com/)

### OpenAI's Former Research Chief Raises $70M to Automate Manufacturing With AI
OpenAI's former chief research officer Bob McGrew raised $70 million to launch a startup called Arda to automate manufacturing. The company is developing a platform that uses video models to analyze factory footage and train robots to operate autonomously. The funding, valuing the startup at $700 million, aims to make Western manufacturing cost-effective and reduce reliance on China.
Source: [msn.com](https://msn.com/)
